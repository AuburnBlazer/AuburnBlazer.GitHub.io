Frequently Asked Questions

1. How are these rankings calculated?

There are 6 main components: 1. Team Record / winning percentage 2. Opponents winning percentage 3. Conference's overall record (in non-conference play) 4. Strength of victory 5. Strength of defeat 6. Score differential

2. Who maintains the project?

The project is maintained by Auburn Blazer on Twitter.

3. Why is [insertTeamHere] above [insertOtherTeamHere]?

Check how each of the teams compare based on the criteria discussed on question #1. If one or more things stick out, that is probably the answer
